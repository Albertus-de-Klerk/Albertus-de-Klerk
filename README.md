<h1>Projects</h1>

<p>
This repository contains a collection of engineering, simulation, and software development projects 
focused on high-performance systems, motorsport applications, and computational modelling.
</p>

<h2>Core Areas</h2>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Simulation & Modelling:</strong> High-fidelity modelling of dynamic systems, including vehicle performance, aerodynamic behaviour, and optimisation frameworks.</li>
    <li><strong>Race Strategy & Performance Engineering:</strong> Development of telemetry-driven models and optimisation tools for race strategy, endurance performance, and real-time decision support.</li>
    <li><strong>CFD & Aerodynamic Analysis:</strong> Custom CFD tooling, solver development, and post-processing pipelines for aerodynamic performance evaluation and workflow automation.</li>
    <li><strong>Data Systems & Telemetry:</strong> Design of data pipelines, telemetry acquisition systems, and real-time analysis tools for motorsport applications.</li>
    <li><strong>Control Systems & Vehicle Software:</strong> Development of onboard control systems and energy management software for high-performance vehicles.</li>
    <li><strong>Software Architecture & Optimisation:</strong> Design and implementation of scalable, high-performance software systems with a focus on computational efficiency and maintainability.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Notable Projects</h2>
<ul>
    <li>CFD solver architecture redesign and optimisation (Rust-based)</li>
    <li>Formula 1 race strategy optimisation model (MILP-based)</li>
    <li>Endurance race strategy and telemetry systems (solar racing)</li>
    <li>Aerodynamic geometry optimisation for racing sailplanes</li>
    <li>Real-time race-data processing and visualisation tools</li>
    <li>CFD automation pipelines for aerodynamic workflows</li>
    <li>Vehicle control and energy management systems</li>
    <li>Hypercar telemetry and trackside sensor data systems</li>
</ul>
